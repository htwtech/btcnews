# 5 Opportunities to Put Your BTC to Work

Here are 5 opportunities to put your BTC to work, ranging from straightforward lending to leveraged liquidity strategies. There's something for every risk appetite.

## 1. BTC.b on Benqi Finance (APY 24.23%)
Supply BTC.b to the lending market to earn yield from borrower interest and protocol incentives. Deposited BTC.b becomes available for borrowing across Avalanche DeFi, and suppliers receive a share of the interest generated.

## 2. Threshold Network tBTC liquidity (APY 3.73%)
The Noon tBTC Vault deploys tBTC into sUSN via minting and staking, then applies measured leverage loops to increase yield. Execution and risk controls are managed directly within the strategy.

## 3. bfBTC Vault (APY 6.3%)
The Vault allows users to stake bfBTC to earn yield generated at the protocol level. Rewards accrue directly to stakers while assets remain within the BitFi vault structure, rather than being actively managed by the user.

## 4. Acre Yield Vault (APY 11%)
A BTC-native vault combining liquidity strategies, options yield, and Bitcoin L2 staking in a single compounding flow. Execution and rebalancing are automated, with rewards converted back into BTC.

## 5. Ekubo WBTC/USDC via Troves (APY 63.15%)
Very high returns still. An automated WBTC/USDC liquidity strategy on Ekubo, with active rebalancing and auto-compounded fees. You receive an ERC-20 vault token representing your share.
