# Feb 25, 2026 | Digest: institutional accumulation, ETF flows, price action + adoption dynamics

River published its Bitcoin Adoption Report 2025: institutions and corporations accumulated ~829,000 BTC during the year across businesses, funds, ETFs and governments. RIAs recorded net buying for eight consecutive quarters, averaging ~$1.5B per quarter into BTC ETFs.

Post-halving annual issuance in 2025 was ~164,250 BTC. On net, institutional absorption equaled roughly 5× new annual supply.  
Allocation mandates and treasury programs operate on periodic review cycles and defined exposure frameworks. The 829k BTC moved primarily into balance sheets and structured vehicles with quarterly allocation routines.

U.S. spot Bitcoin ETFs recorded ~$257.3–257.7M in net inflows yesterday, one of the largest daily inflow figures in recent weeks. Flows were led by multiple issuers. Total AUM had contracted roughly 30% since January (~$117B → ~$81B) before returning to net positive territory at mid-$60k price levels.

## Technical backdrop

There is renewed discussion of a potential multi-day “death cross” as the 50-day simple moving average approaches the 200-day moving average. Historically, similar configurations have appeared in late-cycle phases. Confirmation would require continued convergence of the two averages in coming sessions.
