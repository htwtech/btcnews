# AI models reject fiat, Bitcoin as reserve asset + adoption signal

The Bitcoin Policy Institute ran one of the most revealing experiments in monetary history - and almost no one talked about it.

They gave 36 frontier AI models complete freedom to choose any monetary instrument across 28 financial scenarios. Savings. Payments. Cross-border settlement. Long-term reserves. Models from Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, DeepSeek, xAI, and MiniMax. Over 9,000 individual scenarios tested.

The result: 22 out of 36 models chose crypto as their primary monetary tool. Not one model chose fiat.

Bitcoin captured 79.1% of store-of-value responses. Fiat captured 6%. A two-tier system emerged organically, Bitcoin for savings and reserves, stablecoins for payments. No one designed it, the models reasoned their way there independently.

### Why this matters more than a price update

AI agents aren't theoretical anymore. They're executing trades, managing treasuries, running autonomous protocols.

If the systems making real economic decisions already reject fiat by default - based purely on logical reasoning about monetary properties, we're not watching adoption happen.

We're watching the foundation get built.

The more capable the model, the stronger the Bitcoin preference. That correlation alone should be the headline.

*Source: moneyforai.org - Bitcoin Policy Institute*
