# BTC Ecosystem Weekly Digest

The market is living in two parallel realities right now.
Whales are getting secure institutional vaults, while retail is farming airdrops just by chatting with AI.
Here is the alpha 👇

## 1️⃣ SuperEarn for whales by Avalon Finance
They launched a vault targeting 8-15% APY that doesn't care where Bitcoin goes. Capital is routed through algo-trading and RWAs, and the smart contract automatically cuts losses during wild volatility.
Perfect place to park heavy bags and not lose sleep over drawdowns.

## 2️⃣ Farming with prompts via OP_NET
The "vibe coding" challenge is live for 3 weeks. You type out what you want, and a local AI writes a Bitcoin L1 dApp for you.
Zero code required. They are giving away 250M $PILL and NFTs. Great way to farm ecosystem activity using someone else's brain (the AI).

## 3️⃣ Institutional yield push from Stacks
The Bitcoin Yield Summit is happening on March 11.
The entire narrative is built around showing Wall Street how to safely squeeze yield from their dormant BTC. The ecosystem is actively prepping to absorb serious corporate liquidity.

## 4️⃣ Last call for Midl VibeHack
The hackathon for those who know how to bully Claude into writing smart contracts closes today.
Late entries are accepted but will face a points penalty. If you planned to drop some AI-generated code on them for a chance at the prize pool, wrap it up by tonight.

## 5️⃣ Citrea snapshot is looming
Week 3 of mainnet. The team is tracking everything: volume, lending, and specific dApp usage.
They casually leaked that the final snapshot is expected "before April 2026." Sybil filters are active, so route your transactions like a real human. Time is ticking.

## 6️⃣ Spicenet wraps up Devnet
Spice Flow x Elitra testnet closed with 11k users and 50k transactions.
They successfully stress-tested their EIP-7702 bridging logic. The UX is getting buttery smooth, and they are officially shifting focus to the mainnet launch.

## 7️⃣ BOB crosses 560k users
Just a fat milestone of active wallets. Proves that retail actually wants to use Bitcoin DeFi if you don't make them suffer through terrible UX.

## 8️⃣ SatPay beta invites are out
The Core DAO ecosystem project started sending out invites for the Founders Card.
The era of infinite testnets is fading. Crypto is moving into physical plastic you can actually use to buy coffee. Check your inboxes.
